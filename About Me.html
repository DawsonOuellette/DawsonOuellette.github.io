Dawson Ouellette

I am a passionate musician who has specialized in drum-set/percussion for nearly a decade. In addition, I have took up other instuments such as bass guitar, electrick Guitar, and keyboard. Over the years I learned the importance of having a musical mentor and I have taken professional instruction for the last seven years (and still going). Ever since I started learning from my mentor, my progress accelerated and this event has inspired me to help others acheive the same results.
